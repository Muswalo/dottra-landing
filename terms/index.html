The main service rules for using Dottra account, credit, repayment, and transaction approval features.

Last updated June 6, 2026

Using Dottra

Dottra provides mobile access to credit, repayment, notification, and account-management features for eligible users.

You are responsible for keeping your account details accurate and for protecting your login credentials and transaction approval methods.

Eligibility

Access to credit features may depend on identity verification, account standing, repayment history, and internal risk controls.

Dottra may pause or restrict specific capabilities when account controls, verification issues, or operational requirements apply.

Credit Draws

Before confirming a draw, review the principal amount, fixed charges, repayment amount, and repayment timing shown in the app.

A draw is submitted only after transaction approval is completed with the available approval method for your account.

Repayments

Repayment details shown in the app are provided to help you track your account, but final account records are maintained by Dottra systems.

You should contact Dottra if a repayment, mandate, or account balance appears incorrect.

Changes

Dottra may update these terms as products, policies, or legal requirements change.

Material changes should be reviewed before continuing to use affected services.
